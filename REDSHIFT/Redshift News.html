Save The Date! - Redshift Playing at The Exchange in Bristol

This is for demo purposes - the actual content will be different when the website goes live. The purpose of this blog/news section is to increase SEO by allowing the website to target a wider variety of keywords as searches for many of the main keywords may not be enough to generate a large following on their own. These words such as progressive metal tend to have high competition and low searches. By talking about events that the band are attending we can also attach the band to the local area. This will when people search for local bands to book for specific events in the area.
