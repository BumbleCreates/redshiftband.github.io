Redshift About

Redshift is a Bath based progressive metal trio.

Band Members

Liam Fear - Vocals, Bass, Keyboards

Tiago Martins - Guitar

Jack Camp - Drums

Previous Members

Josua Boniface (2019-2023) - Guitar

Albums

Redshift band have released two progressive metal concept albums. Cataclysm (released in 2019) was the bands debut album. The album concept was centred around an alien invasion. In 2023 Redshift released their second album The Laws of Entrophy. This album explored a journey through life from start to finish, discovering how life does not always come easily. It has dark undertones and focuses on struggles, mental health, the ups and downs of life. The progressive metal trio has been compared to bands such as Dream Theater, Between The Burried And Me & Rush. In 2023 Tiago Martains joined the trio and a new concept metal album is currently being worked on.
